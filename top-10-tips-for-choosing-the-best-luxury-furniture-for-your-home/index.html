What Sets Luxury Furniture Apart?

Understanding what distinguishes elite furniture from the mass-market alternatives is crucial for any professional in the furnishings industry. The top characteristics that define high-end furniture include:

  • Materials: Rare hardwoods, full-grain Italian leathers, natural marbles, and hand-polished metals.
  • Craftsmanship: Pieces often involve hundreds of hours of craftsmanship, often incorporating hand-carving, marquetry, and intricate upholstery.
  • Design Legacy: Authored by internationally recognized designers or inspired by historical art movements like Baroque, Rococo, or Mid-Century Modern.
