Luxury embraces balance. Whether you're working with a twin, king, or canopy bed, the placement of bedside tables, lighting, and mirrors should reflect symmetry. It creates visual calm and instills a sense of order.

Flow & Proportion

Too many bulky or oversized pieces can crowd a bedroom, disrupting relaxation. Ensure each furniture item's scale aligns with the room‚Äôs proportions. Leave at least 2 feet around each major piece for uninterrupted circulation.
