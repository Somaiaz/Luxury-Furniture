Evaluating Craftsmanship and Authenticity

Professionals with years in the industry know what separates factory output from master-level furniture. Here are a few markers to evaluate:

  • Joinery: Dovetail joints, mortise and tenon, and proper reinforcements signal expert construction.
  • Finish: Surfaces should gleam smoothly under natural light without bubbling or rough strokes.
  • Fabric and Upholstery: High thread count textiles, ornate piping, tufting done by hand, and leather sourced from certified tanneries.
  • Origin Certifications: Especially for wood such as walnut, oak, and rosewood, ethical sourcing matters, as do carving origins like Tuscany or Veneto.
