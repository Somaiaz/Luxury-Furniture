  • Torque: The engine generates significant torque, often reaching up to 600 Nm, which is crucial for heavy-duty hauling.
  • Transmission: This vehicle usually comes with a 6-speed manual transmission, providing drivers with smooth gear transitions essential for varying road conditions.
  • Payload Capacity: The Hyundai HD 610 boasts a payload capacity of up to 6,500 kg, making it suitable for transporting heavy loads.
